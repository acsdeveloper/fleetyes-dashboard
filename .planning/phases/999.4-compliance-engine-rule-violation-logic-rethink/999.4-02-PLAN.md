# Plan 02: Date Attribution Fix + Prospective Check Cleanup

## Objective
Fix the three aggregate rules in `assimilated.ts` that emit violations with the wrong date
(the week-start Monday `ws` instead of the specific working day). Also remove diagnostic
`console.warn` spam from `prospective-check.ts` and enrich its violations with trip context.

After this plan, rota cells will correctly show weekly driving, fortnightly driving, and
weekly rest violations because `v.date` will match the actual working day being displayed.

## must_haves
- `EU_WEEKLY_DRIVE_LIMIT` violation `date` equals the last working-day in the week
  whose driving pushed the cumulative total over 3360 minutes (56 hours) — NOT `ws`
- `EU_FORTNIGHTLY_DRIVE_LIMIT` violation `date` equals the last working-day in the
  fortnight whose driving pushed the cumulative total over 5400 minutes (90 hours) — NOT `ws`
- `EU_WEEKLY_REST` violation `date` equals the first non-rest working-day in the
  deficient week — NOT `ws`
- `EU_CONSECUTIVE_REDUCED_WEEKLY_REST` violation `date` equals the first non-rest
  working-day in the second deficient week — NOT `ws`
- Zero `console.warn` calls in `prospective-check.ts`
- All prospective violations include `tripId`, `tripStartTime`, `tripEndTime`
- All 71 existing tests still pass
